Frequently Asked Questions


Is there a shipping option for overseas?


  -Unfortunately, there is no shipping for overseas in Relohan sa Pasig. Maybe in the future.


Is there payment methods other than COD?


  -No, other payment methods are not supported as of now.


Can I cancel if the order is verified?


  -No, you can only cancel if the order is still pending.


When will the product I want to buy be available?


  -It depends on the supplier. Please wait patiently.


The address I placed is incorrect, can I change it?


  -As long as the status of the order is pending, you can press the cancel button.
